About This Project

This application uses a trained machine learning model to estimate the probability of cardiovascular disease based on clinical metrics. By analyzing historical patient data, the algorithm identifies complex patterns across multiple risk factors to provide a rapid, data-driven health assessment.

297 Patients
13 Features
80.5% Accuracy
Data: UCI Heart Disease Dataset, Cleveland Clinic 1988 View Source
Built with Python, scikit-learn, Flask

For educational and informational purposes only. This tool does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
